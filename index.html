🌟 Project Overview

Peace and Partnership is a meme coin designed with simplicity and community in mind. With a total supply of 1,618,000,000 tokens — inspired by the Fibonacci Golden Ratio — we’re building a community-driven ecosystem that values trust, transparency, and a fair launch.

🔑 Key Features

  • 1% Buy & Sell Tax – directed to the Developer to sustain the project and grow the ecosystem.
  • No Dev Tokens – The Developer holds no initial tokens to show unwavering faith in the project’s future.
  • Community-First – The contract will be renounced to build trust and empower the community.

🔥 Token Burn Strategy

Once the project’s tax revenue reaches 2 million USDT, 60% of the tax revenue will be used to burn tokens, reducing supply and driving scarcity. After reaching this milestone, monthly burns will occur at any time to further support the token’s deflationary future.
